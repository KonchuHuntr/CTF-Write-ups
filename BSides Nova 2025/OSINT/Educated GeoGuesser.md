# Educated GeoGuesser
What is the name of the river flowing through the valley behind [these rock formations](https://metaproblems.com/c10c109fef2eefc1cdde7e4cc83072a4/nature.jpg)?

Simply enter the name of the river. The grader is lenient.

# Solution
Starting off with reverse image search we find out its Badlands National park. With a quick google search we find out its the While River.
